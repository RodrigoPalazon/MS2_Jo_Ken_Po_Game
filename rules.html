JO-KEN-PO Battle

JO-KEN-PO Rules:

Jokenpo is a famous battle game where the player has three options of power: Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

How to play Jo_Ken_Po_Battle:
  • Choose the level of difficulty that you would like to play:
    • EASY: Win the player that makes 3 points first
    • MEDIUM: Win the player that makes 5 points first
    • HARD: Win the player that makes 9 points first
  • Making Points:
    • Rock breaks Scissors.
    • Scissors cuts Paper.
    • Paper involves Rock.
    • Tied battle increases no point for the players
  • Winning the Jo_Ken_Po_Battle:
    • The first player making the maximum points referent to the difficulty's level chosen won the Jo_Ken_Po_Battle.
  • Restarting a new Jo_Ken_Po_Battle.
    • After Winning or Losing a Jo_Ken_Po_Battle, three buttons will be display in your screen.
      • Play Again: to starting a new Battle.
      • Rules: to checking the rules.
      • Quit: to returning to the initial menu.
